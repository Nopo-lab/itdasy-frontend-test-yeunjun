5. Data Deletion

  • You may delete your account at any time via Settings → Logout → Delete Account in the app, or by calling DELETE /auth/delete-account.
  • Account deletion triggers immediate erasure of personal data. Items retained by legal obligation (e.g., payment records for 5 years) are anonymized.
  • Meta's data deletion callback endpoint: here

6. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your data (GET /auth/export in-app).
  • Rectification — Edit your profile in Settings.
  • Erasure — Delete your account (as described in §5).
  • Data portability — Export in JSON format (GET /auth/export).
  • Objection / Restriction — Contact contact@itdasy.com.
  • EU users — You may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
  • California users — You have the right to know, delete, and opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal data).

7. Security Measures

  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit (HSTS enforced)
  • Passwords hashed with bcrypt
  • Instagram access tokens encrypted with Fernet (AES-128-CBC + HMAC-SHA256)
  • Supabase Postgres encryption at rest
  • Role-based access control; only authorized engineers can query the database
  • IP-based rate limiting on login (10 attempts per 5 minutes)

8. AI-Generated Content

  • Captions and images generated by AI are provided on a best-effort basis. Users must review and verify accuracy before publishing.
  • The Company is not liable for damages arising from publication of AI-generated content that violates local advertising, medical, or food safety laws.
  • Under Korean law, works generated solely by AI without meaningful human authorship may have limited copyright protection. Users acknowledge this limitation.

9. Cookies and Local Storage

We use localStorage for authentication tokens and user preferences (no third-party tracking cookies). You may clear these via browser settings or by logging out.
